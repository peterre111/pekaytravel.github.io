Our History

Travelling and tourism provide an opportunity to meet and make friends, experience other cultures and broaden your worldview, so as to be a citizen of the world who appreciates other people. We welcome all travellers to Kenya. Our country offers amazing options of experiences for the discerning leisure, business, nature, cultural and eco-sensitive travellers. Our people are most warm and friendly and our commitment to sustainable tourism is unrivalled. It is, therefore, incumbent on every one of us in government, private sector and all Kenyans to place our best foot forward to preserve and promote this national heritage

The site was created by an undergraduate student Kamau Kariuki,who was pursuing a degree in Computer Science which is a key figure in the history of online travel.The site was conceived primarily as a showcase for what a Travel company did behind the scenes, but its uniqueness made it popular with many early visitors to the web.

The site was built mainly to display Kenyan tourist attraction sites which have been of great impact to the Tourism industry.It briefly showcases some of the key Prehistoric sites that both local people and forign people visit mostl.It also provides room for booking of restaurants and resorts for Tourists.
